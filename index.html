Change username
Your profile link becomes visrodeck.com/@newname once confirmed by email.
Migrate account
Move this account — and everything tied to it — to a different email address.
Request my collected data
Confirm by email, then we'll send a download link with everything we hold on your account.
Delete account
Permanently deletes your account once confirmed. Workspaces you own transfer or get deleted first.
